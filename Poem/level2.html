Last night, while I lay
thinking
here,

















                                                                                                                                                                         Some

                                                                                                                                         Whatifs

                                                                                                                                                                         crawled inside my ear,

















And pranced and partied

All night long


















                                                                                                                                                                         And sang their same old

                                                                                                                                                                Whatif

                                                                                                                                                                         song:

















WHATIF










  • I'm dumb in school?









  • they've closed the swimming pool?









  • I get beat up?









  • there's poison in my cup?









  • I start to cry?









  • I get sick and die?









  • I flunk that test?









  • green hair grows on my chest?









  • nobody likes me?









  • a bolt of lightning strikes me?









  • I don't grow taller?









  • my head starts getting smaller?









  • the fish won't bite?









  • the wind tears up my kite?









  • they start a war?









  • my parents get divorced?









  • the bus is late?









  • my teeth don't grow in straight?









  • I tear my pants?









  • I never learn to dance?

















Everything seems swell, and then

The nighttime Whatifs strike again!
